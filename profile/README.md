### Overview

Sid Meier’s Civilization VII is a turn-based strategy game focused on building empires, managing resources, exploring technologies, and competing against other civilizations across different eras of history.

The game emphasizes long-term planning, diplomacy, warfare strategy, and economic development.

### Core Gameplay Elements

- Turn-based empire building  
- City development and expansion  
- Technology and research progression  
- Diplomacy and alliances  
- Strategic warfare systems  
- Resource management  
- Victory condition strategies
